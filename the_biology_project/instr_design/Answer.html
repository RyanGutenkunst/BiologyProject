The Answer and Correct Answer Documents

The Answer and Correct Answer documents are identical, except for the fact that the Correct document has a large Correct! under the title of the problem.

On those documents we repeat the question and the possible answers, but this time the correct answer and its brief indented explanation are in color to clearly separate them from the alternative answers.
