Expansion that will benefit the General Public

The expanded Biology Project will include topics that everyone should understand to live a healthy and contributing life in today's world. We will offer additional topics such as nutrition and obesity, smoking and cancer, and gene cloning.

The general public will have no-cost access to all levels of The Biology Project. Anyone can expand on their existing knowledge of biology .
