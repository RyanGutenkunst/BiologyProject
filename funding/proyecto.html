El Proyecto Biologico

The site is widely used by bilingual students and the Spanish-speaking. It was partly translated into Spanish at:

  • Universidad Nacional de Formosa, Formosa, Argentina
  • Universidad de Chile, Santiago, Chile
The translation will be completed with funding from:
  • the NSF Recognition Award for the Integration of Research & Education
