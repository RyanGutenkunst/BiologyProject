Problem 1: Recognition of the peptide bond



Which arrow points to a peptide bond?
A. 1
B. 2
C. 2 and 3
D. 3
E. 4 and 5
Arrow 4 denotes the peptide bond between the first and second amino acid, and arrow 5 marks the peptide bond joining the fourth and fifth amino acid.
