Problem 8 Tutorial: Equation for light reaction

Which of the following is the equation for the light reaction of photosynthesis?

Summary of equations for the light reactions

During the light reactions, water is oxidized, electrons are transferred from water to NADP+, and ATP is synthesized. The three components of the light reaction can be summarized with equations:

Oxidation of water
2 H2O 02 + 4e- + 4 H+

Reduction of NADP+
2 NADP+ + 4e- + 4 H+ 2 NADPH + 2 H+

ATP synthesis
ADP + Pi ATP
When these equations are combined, the overall equation for the light reactions is obtained.

2 H2O + 2 NADP+ + nADP + nPi + hv O2 + 2 NADPH + 2 H+ + nATP
