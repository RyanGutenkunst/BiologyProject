Problem 4: Cytochalasins

Cytochalasins are drugs that interfere with actin polymerization into microfilaments. If you add cytochalasin to cultured mammalian cells that have just begun mitosis what is most likely to happen?

C. The cells will complete mitosis and arrest at cytokinesis.
Correct.
Microfilaments are needed for cytokinesis.
