Problem 11. Lack of an immune response to self

Correct!

The lack of an immune response to self is called:

A. Autoimmunity
B.

Tolerance

Tolerance is the “opposite” of autoimmunity. Autoimmunity is an immune response to self, and tolerance is the lack of that response.
