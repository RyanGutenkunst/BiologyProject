Population Set-up

To begin, enter a population size (from 10 to 30, even numbers only), and the number of individuals initially HIV+.

If you will be providing any names of individuals, please enter them in the spaces provided. For greater display variability, you may place the names in any of the boxes. However, if you put a name in a box with a number greater than the total number of individuals, that name will not appear.

You may provide as many as 30 names, although you do not have to provide any – the simulation program has a list of 30 names to use and will fill in any blanks. Here is the included name list, in case you have people with the same names:
