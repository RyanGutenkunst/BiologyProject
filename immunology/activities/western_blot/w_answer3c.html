Question 3

In general, a second antibody or "conjugate"...
Binds primary antibody
CORRECT!
The conjugate or second antibody, binds to primary antibody, in general. If one were performing a test for cytomegalovirus (CMV), the second antibody would still bind to primary antibody, but those primary antibodies would be anti-CMV antibodies.
