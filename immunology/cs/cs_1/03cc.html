What does DPT stand for?

A. Diphtheria, Pertussis, Trichomonas
B. Diphtheria, Pseudomonas, Tetanus
C. Damsela, Pertussis, Tetanus
C. Diphtheria, Pertussis, Tetanus
This vaccination protects against infections by Corynebacterium diphtheriae, Bordetella pertussis (which causes whooping cough), and Clostridium tetani.
