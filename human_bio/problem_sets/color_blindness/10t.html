Color Blindness Problem Set

Problem 10: Audrei's sons

What is the probability that Audrei's sons will be color blind?

A. 25%
B. 50%
C. 75%
D. 100%

Tutorial

Regardless of whether the father of Audrei's children has normal vision or the color perception defect, all of her sons will inherit one of her X-chromosomes which both carry the color perception defect.
