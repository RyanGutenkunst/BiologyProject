Problem 6: Pedigree representation for Paul

Paul, like Liz, is color blind. How will he be represented in the family pedigree?

A.
B.

To be color blind, Paul had to have inherited an X-chromosome with the red-green color blind allele from his mother and a Y chromosome from his father. Therefore, he is represented by the symbol of the males affected with the color blindness.
