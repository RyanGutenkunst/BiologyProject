Problem 5: STD Present in Most Diagnosed Cervical Cancer

Which of the following sexually transmitted diseases is present in most diagnosed cervical cancers?

A. Herpes
B. Human papilloma virus (HPV)
Human papilloma virus (HPV), the virus that cause genital and anal warts, is a risk factor for cervical cancer. It is present in 97% of the diagnosed cervical cancers
